About Me

My name is Márcia Tavares and I’m a UX/UI Researcher & Designer from Portugal 🇵🇹 currently based in Amsterdam. My end goal is to create products that promote effective and valuable experiences for the end user. You will find me working with cross-functional teams, driving user requirements from concept to release – doing user research, prototyping and testing.

I have a background in Computer Engineer and I hold a master degree in Design Multimedia which allows me to fill in the gap between design and development. I tend to be involved in the development phase as well putting my hands into code 👩‍💻. However I can say that my work lies on the intersection between business, technology and design. I have a critical, pragmatic and collaborative approach when it comes to create new products.

I really love what I do and I like to learn more and more about it everyday so if you wanna discuss a specific project or just know more about me please don’t hesitate to reach out to me.
